Manage Assigned Learners

Monitor your assessment responsibilities effortlessly with our user-friendly dashboards. The main page displays a clear list of all qualifications assigned to you as an assessor.

Clicking on any qualification reveals a detailed view of all learners assigned to you, including key information such as Learner Name, Progress, Pending Assessments, IQA Name, and IQA Sampling Ratio (%). This streamlined overview provides instant insight into each learner's assessment progress.

Conduct Assessments

Effortlessly review learner submissions and make informed assessment decisions with our user-friendly dashboard. Evaluate each submission against the VACSR criteria.

Mark submissions as Accepted or select Resubmission Required with a single click. For streamlined efficiency, feedback is linked to each Learning Outcome, eliminating the need to provide detailed comments for every assessment criterion individually.

Record Assessment Outcomes

Efficiently record and manage your assessment decisions with our intuitive system. Capture your evaluations effortlessly and review them anytime.

Review comprehensive history of feedback provided for each Learning Outcome. Track exactly what was expected from learners when you marked their work as "Resubmission Required," ensuring clarity and consistency.

Receive IQA Feedback

Enhance your assessment skills with our comprehensive IQA feedback system. All Internal Quality Assurance feedback on your assessment decisions is meticulously recorded.

Dive into historical records to explore detailed feedback, including who provided it, when it was given, and whether it pertains to interim or summative sampling. This rich data empowers you to reflect on your decisions and identify areas for improvement.

Track Assessment Progress

Stay informed and in control with our intuitive assessor dashboard. From the main dashboard, you can instantly view all submissions pending assessment.

Dive deeper to monitor each learner's assessment progress with detailed insights, including completion status and pending actions. The clear, organized layout highlights key details, allowing you to track progress effortlessly and prioritize your efforts.
