Keep Track of Your Assessment Journey

Stay on top of your learning journey with our intuitive progress tracking system. The dynamic progress bar displays your completion percentage in real time, giving you a clear and motivating view of your progress.

Each assessment criterion is clearly listed with its current status. Easily track which tasks have been submitted, accepted, require resubmission, or are still pending. This transparent breakdown empowers you to focus your efforts and stay organized.

Upload Evidence Submission with Ease

Effortlessly submit your evidence with our user-friendly system. Prepare your files in advance—whether they're MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, PDFs, videos, or images—and upload them with ease.

Simply select the relevant assessment criterion, click "Upload Evidence," and submit your files. Ensure each file is under 50MB for smooth upload. Made a mistake? No problem! You can easily replace submissions.

View Assessor Feedback & Historical Records

Stay in control with our user-friendly feedback system. Each submission is accompanied by detailed assessor feedback, allowing you to confirm quality or address specific concerns.

Review assessor names for transparency and track comments to refine your approach. Access complete submission history to monitor progress over time, identifying patterns and growth opportunities.

Instant Notifications for Assessment Decisions

Stay informed with our real-time assessment notification system. Receive instant alerts the moment an assessor marks your assessments, keeping you updated without delay.

Each notification provides clear outcomes of assessment decisions. Whether accepted, requiring resubmission, or under review, every update is displayed prominently in your notification panel.

Submit Mandatory Documents Hassle-Free

Take charge with our streamlined document submission tab. A clear, centralized list displays all required documents, ensuring you know exactly what's needed to move forward.

Each document's status is updated in real time. See which have been submitted, pending review, or require resubmission. Detailed outcomes from assessor reviews appear in relevant sections.
