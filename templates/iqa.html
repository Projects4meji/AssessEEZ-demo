Monitor Assessor Performance

Ensure the integrity of the assessment process with our robust quality assurance system. Review assessment decisions made by assessors to maintain high standards.

Each decision is evaluated and marked as either "Accepted" or "Non-Conformity." An "Accepted" mark reassures assessors of their accurate judgments, while a "Non-Conformity" mark prompts re-assessment, guiding assessors to address discrepancies and align with quality standards.

Elevate your assessment process with our robust feedback system. As an IQA, you can provide interim sampling feedback or summative sampling feedback.

Our intuitive platform makes it easy to document and share constructive insights, fostering continuous improvement in assessment practices. Each feedback entry is clearly organized, allowing you to track its status and impact over time.
