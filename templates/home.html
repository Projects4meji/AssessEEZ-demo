How It Works

Discover the seamless four-step process that makes AssessEEZ the perfect solution for your assessment needs

1

Setup and Assignment

In the initial step, admins utilize AssessEEZ to set up qualifications, either by selecting from a predefined list or by creating new ones tailored to specific requirements. This process is followed by the assignment of relevant users (assessors, IQAs, EQAs, and learners) to these qualifications, ensuring a structured and organized approach from the outset.

Admin Dashboard

  • Create or select qualifications
  • Assign users to roles
  • Structured organization
2

Learner Engagement

Learners then engage with the platform, accessing their personalized dashboard to view assigned qualifications and track their progress. This phase is characterized by the submission of evidence against assessment criteria, with learners able to monitor the status of their submissions in real-time, facilitating a more interactive and responsive experience.

Learner Dashboard

  • Personalized dashboard access
  • Real-time progress tracking
  • Evidence submission
3

Assessment and Review

Assessors take center stage in this step, utilizing the platform's tools to review learner submissions and documents. This comprehensive review process is aided by access to resource materials and IQA reports, enabling assessors to provide detailed, actionable feedback to learners.

Assessment Tools

  • Review learner submissions
  • Access resource materials
  • Provide detailed feedback
4

Quality Assurance

In the final step, Internal Quality Assurers (IQAs) utilize AssessEEZ to review and provide feedback on assessor decisions, ensuring a rigorous quality control process. This oversight enables the identification of areas for improvement, contributing to the continuous enhancement of the assessment process. IQAs can also upload reports for both assessors and learners, with controlled visibility to maintain confidentiality where necessary.

Quality Control

  • Review assessor decisions
  • Provide feedback
  • Continuous improvement
